About the Tiger

Tigers (Panthera tigris) are powerful, solitary hunters found across parts of Asia. They prefer forests and grasslands and hunt deer, wild boar, and other medium-to-large prey.

Each tiger’s stripe pattern is unique—like a fingerprint. Conservation work has helped some populations stabilize, but habitat loss and poaching remain major threats.
