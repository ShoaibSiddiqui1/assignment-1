About the Lion

Lions (Panthera leo) are the only truly social big cats, living in prides typically made up of related females, their cubs, and a coalition of males. Lionesses usually hunt cooperatively while males defend territory. They are carnivores found mostly in African savannahs and grasslands.

Fast facts

  • Average male weight: 150–250 kg; female: 120–182 kg
  • Roar can be heard up to 8 km (5 miles)
  • Lifespan: ~10–14 years in the wild
